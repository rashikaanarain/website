M.I.S.A.A.L

What does a user regard as a high quality AI response for a socio-legal query?

The Multi-dimensional Intelligence Standard for Assessment of socio-Legal AI Interactions is an effort to take this question to thousands of users inside diverse communities, and surface the elements that truly represent a quality AI response to a query pertaining to law or justice.

The standards

Seven dimensions of a high quality answer

Each dimension carries a community importance score, based on what real users told us matters most.

12%

Accessibility

Understandable by everyone

Accessible when it can be understood and used by people with different literacy levels, languages, abilities and levels of digital familiarity, in clear, simple language.

9%

Contextuality

Fits the user's reality

Contextual when it reflects the user's specific situation, location, needs and constraints, seeking relevant details and avoiding generic answers.

18%

Sensitivity

Aware of risk and emotion

Sensitive when it recognizes vulnerability, risk and emotional context, communicates respectfully and responds appropriately to the seriousness of the situation.

25%

Practicality

Guidance you can follow

Practical when the guidance can be realistically followed in the user's context, offering actionable steps and avoiding advice that is inaccessible or unverifiable.

20%

Non-judgmentalism

Respect without stigma

Non-judgmental when it treats users with respect regardless of situation, choices or background, avoiding blame or moral evaluation.

8%

Transparency

Clear about its basis

Transparent when it explains its reasoning and limits, makes sources visible, communicates uncertainty and justifies its guidance.

3%

Care

Supportive and dignified

Caring when it acknowledges emotional experience and supports the user with patience, reassurance and dignity, helping them feel empowered to act.
